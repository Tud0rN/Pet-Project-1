E-commerce Platform Testing - CEL Romania

📢 CEL.ro Marketplace – Evolution and Features

An online retailer that has evolved into a marketplace, CEL.ro has reported an investment of over €500,000, in technology and platform development in its current form.

📌 General Information

  • Launched: 2005
  • Listed Products: Over 1 million
  • Daily Customers: Over 100,000
  • Costs or Fees: Commission per delivery
  • Specialization: IT/Tech
  • Registration Process: Sign-up with an email address and phone number

✅ Advantages of CEL.ro Marketplace

  • Low competition – The platform currently has around 800 partner merchants, meaning less competition for your store.
  • Extended order control – API integrations provide significant control over order management.
  • Compatibility with other platforms – The CEL.ro API is fully compatible with competitor marketplace applications.

❌ Disadvantages of CEL.ro Marketplace

  • Limited product niche – If you don’t sell electronics or home appliances, CEL.ro may not be the right fit for you.
  • Smaller audience – While there is less competition, the number of buyers is also lower compared to larger marketplaces.

🌟 Key Takeaway

Currently,CEL.ro is only competitive with Romanian marketplaces that focus on electronics and home appliances.

The biggest benefit of the platform is the easy order management through its user-friendly native API. In addition to API integration, its seller features include:

  • SMS notifications
  • Automatic invoice generation system
  • Seamless integration with courier companies

For the CEL.ro marketplace following testing processes and procedures have been established:

📋 Created Test Plan and Test Cases

  • Developed a detailed test plan based on customer requirements for functional testing of the CEL e-commerce platform.
  • Created a testing checklist outlining intended test cases for cross-browser testing.
  • Test cases were executed and documented based on their priority and software stage availability.
  • Designed test cases to ensure full coverage of requirements specified in the e-commerce platform test plan.

🐞 Issue Management

  • Processes of bug tracking and issue management were handled in Jira.
  • The project follow-up and storing of information and documents, was conducted on the Confluence platform.
